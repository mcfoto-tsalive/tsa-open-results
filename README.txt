TSA Open Championship 2026 Dashboard — Split Open Cards Version

Important scoring change:
- Open score is now entered as two separate 10-shot cards:
  Open Card 1 Score / Open Card 1 Centres
  Open Card 2 Score / Open Card 2 Centres
- The TV still displays only the combined Open 200.20 score.
- If one Open card is 100, the leading '1' in the 200 score is shown red.
- If both Open cards are 100, the whole 200.20 score is shown red.

Use:
1. Export your Numbers score table as CSV.
2. Open dashboard.html.
3. Click Load scores.csv and choose your exported CSV.
4. Full-screen the browser on the TV.

Valid grades:
Master, A, B, C, D

Keyboard:
H hides/shows buttons.
Left/right arrows move screens.


Logo layout update:
- TSA logo enlarged.
- Header layout adjusted so the championship title sits closer to centre.
- Title slightly reduced and centre-aligned for better TV balance.
